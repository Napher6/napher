Analyzing geographical characteristics of straw burning in China during the past 5 years

straw burning; geographical characteristics; spatial heterogeneity

Objectively and precisely depicting the spatial distribution characteristics of straw burning is of great significance to scientifically guide the prohibition of straw burning and improve the efficiency of administrative supervision. Based on Satsee-Fire point data sets in China during 2014 to 2018 year, natural environment and population-economy data, China straw fire points database and nature-population-economics database established by terrain, social and economic data, this study uses GIS analysis tools and mathematical statistics methods to analyze the distribution characteristics of straw burning fire points in China during the recent 5 years under the influences of 9 factors in 3 aspects including terrain, public transport and the population-economy, the results showed that:1) straw burning phenomenon concentrated in areas whose elevation is less than 250 m, gradient is less than 25°, and relief is less than 30m. And the number of fire points whose location is on the north slope or the south slope accounts for 43.32% and 36.63% of the total, respectively. 2) straw burning presents different spatial characteristics in the forbidden area of airports and railways. The farther away from the airport, the more common the straw burning is. Nearly 60% of the straw burning in the forbidden area of the airports takes place 12-15km away from the airport. However, the distribution of straw burning fire points in the forbidden area of railways are more balanced, and the internal circle is slightly more dense than the external region on the whole. In terms of space, the fire point density increases gradually with the increase of latitude. The number of fire points in the forbidden area of railways in Shanxi, Inner Mongolia, Hebei and the three northeast provinces of China accounts for 76.74% of the total. 3) nearly 70% of straw burning occurred in counties with population density less than 250 people /km^2, while 58.22% occurred in counties with annual GDP less than 10 billion yuan; There is a negative correlation between the phenomenon of straw burning and the population density and GDP level of the county, that is, the larger the population density and the more developed the county economy is, the more rare the phenomenon of straw burning is.

Mining Regional Pattern of Land Use with Adaptive Adjacent Criterion

Land use/cover change; regional pattern; spatio-temporal association rule; geographical zone; semi-variogram

Regional patterns of land use can explain social, environment, institutional and economic processes and thus have innumerable research value. Nonetheless, these patterns need great efforts to discover, because of their variances with space owing to spatial heterogeneity resulting from diverse geographic characteristics. Aimed at solving the difficulties above, we proposed a regional pattern mining method to discover spatial-temporal association rules of land use. We also combined geographical zoning and adjacent criterion based on semi-variogram for adaptive mining in different regions. In addition, a combinational decision model was devised to reinforce the stability of rule interestingness measure for sparse dataset of land use change. Using our method, we conducted a case study in Xinbei District, Jiangsu Province, China. According to the characteristics of different locations in study area, six geographical zones were formed. Then dependence of land use and regional patterns of land use change were mined based on spatio-temporal association rule. The results indicated that the typical changes of dependence, which included those among arable land, residential land and water body, varied in different geographical zones. Moreover, each pattern of land use change could be denoted by some kinds of spatio-temporal association rules through two-level mining method. Each divided zones turned to have different land use patterns with different intensity. Result showed that land use parcels of residential land transformed from other land use types in two distinct modes, either close to or far away from existed built-up areas. The former manner of urban and rural growth chiefly happened in township and airport surrounding area, while the latter primarily happened in urban and rural area. Besides, we were capable to discover patterns of transportation infrastructure construction and land rearrangement, which mainly happened in airport surrounding area and rural area, respectively.
